Rimal Semiconductors Secures Strategic Investment from Keheilan Asset Management

From Sand to Silicon: Rimal Semiconductors and Keheilan Unite to Build Saudi Arabia's Semiconductor Industry

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia — March 2026. Keheilan Asset Management is pleased to announce a strategic investment in Rimal Semiconductors, a pioneering Saudi company dedicated to building a domestic semiconductor ecosystem in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The partnership was formalized at Al Diriyah — a location steeped in historical significance — symbolising the deep roots from which this venture grows and the long-term vision it is built to serve.

The signing ceremony brought together Dr. Ahmed Abdelhamid, CEO and Founder of Keheilan Asset Management, and the founding team of Rimal Semiconductors, marking a meaningful milestone in the development of Saudi Arabia's emerging deep technology landscape.

About Rimal Semiconductors

Rimal Semiconductors is a Saudi-founded company with a singular mission: to build a world-class semiconductor industry within the Kingdom. The name "Rimal" — meaning "sands" in Arabic — reflects both the nation's heritage and the raw material at the heart of semiconductor manufacturing: silicon, derived from sand.

Rimal was founded on the conviction that Saudi Arabia possesses the talent, capital, and strategic alignment to develop sovereign semiconductor capabilities — reducing dependency on foreign technology supply chains while positioning the Kingdom as a contributor to the global technology stack. The company is developing capabilities across chip design, fabrication process knowledge, and semiconductor ecosystem development, with a focus on applications relevant to Vision 2030 priorities including smart infrastructure, energy, and defense technology.

Strategic Rationale

For Keheilan Asset Management, this investment reflects our conviction that deep technology ventures in the MENA region represent a generational opportunity. Semiconductor sovereignty is increasingly recognised as a national security and economic priority across the world's leading economies. Saudi Arabia, with its significant capital base, growing engineering talent pool, and clear government commitment to technology localisation, is uniquely positioned to build meaningful capabilities in this space.

"We believe the next chapter of Saudi Arabia's economic transformation will be written in silicon. Rimal Semiconductors represents exactly the kind of bold, long-horizon venture that Keheilan was built to support. We are proud to stand with the Rimal team at the beginning of what we believe will be a defining journey."

— Dr. Ahmed Abdelhamid, CEO & Founder, Keheilan Asset Management

Vision 2030 Alignment

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 program has identified technological self-sufficiency as a core pillar of economic diversification. Semiconductors sit at the intersection of every major technology vertical the Kingdom is investing in — artificial intelligence, smart cities, renewable energy infrastructure, and defense modernisation. Rimal Semiconductors is building for this convergence.

The investment by Keheilan is structured to support Rimal's early-stage development including talent acquisition, R&D infrastructure, and strategic partnerships with global semiconductor research institutions.

How It Works

Choose your risk level. We do everything else.

From construction to continuous rebalancing, every operational aspect is handled by Keheilan. You make one decision — the risk level — and we execute the rest.

01
Choose Your Risk Profile

Select from Conservative, Balanced, Growth, or Aggressive Growth. Each profile maps to a distinct benchmark and volatility range, matched to your investment horizon and objectives.

02
We Build the Portfolio

Keheilan constructs your portfolio from Shariah-compliant securities that replicate the selected benchmark. Every instrument is screened for compliance, quality, and liquidity before inclusion.

03
We Monitor Continuously

Portfolio drift from the benchmark is monitored in real time. Compliance status of each holding is tracked on an ongoing basis. You receive monthly reporting on both performance and compliance.

04
We Rebalance as Needed

When market movements cause the portfolio to drift beyond defined thresholds, we rebalance to restore benchmark alignment without requiring any action from you.

SFDR Disclosure — Article 8 Classification

Keheilan Asset Management is registered in the Netherlands and subject to SFDR (EU) 2019/2088. All strategies are classified as Article 8 — they promote social and ethical characteristics by constructing portfolios within a Shariah-compliant investment universe (excluding interest-bearing instruments, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, and gambling). This is a structural feature of instrument selection, not a post-hoc screen. Keheilan does not commit to a minimum proportion of EU Taxonomy-aligned investments and does not represent its strategies as "sustainable investments" under Article 2(17) of SFDR. Principal adverse impacts (PAIs) are not currently considered at the entity level under Article 4. For more information: invest@keheilan.com

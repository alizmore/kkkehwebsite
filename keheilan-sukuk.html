How It Works

Simple to enter. Professionally managed throughout.

Three steps from decision to a fully managed Sukuk portfolio.

01
Review the Portfolio

The Sukuk Portfolio targets a 4–6% annual return through a single allocation to sovereign Islamic bonds. Review the strategy, fee structure, and return expectations, then invest with full clarity on what you are entering.

02
We Build Your Portfolio

Keheilan selects sovereign Sukuk instruments from sovereign issuers, within a Shariah-compliant investment universe. Instrument maturities are staggered to reduce interest-rate sensitivity and preserve capital.

Income generated by Sukuk instruments is reinvested into the portfolio by default, growing your base position continuously. For investors who prefer distributions, that option is available and set at onboarding.

SFDR Disclosure — Article 8 Classification

Keheilan Asset Management is registered in the Netherlands and subject to SFDR (EU) 2019/2088. All strategies are classified as Article 8 — they promote social and ethical characteristics by constructing portfolios within a Shariah-compliant investment universe (excluding interest-bearing instruments, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, and gambling). This is a structural feature of instrument selection, not a post-hoc screen. Keheilan does not commit to a minimum proportion of EU Taxonomy-aligned investments and does not represent its strategies as "sustainable investments" under Article 2(17) of SFDR.

Why Sukuk Portfolios

Stability is a strategy. Not a compromise.

For investors who cannot expose capital to equity volatility, Sukuk Portfolios provides institutional-grade fixed income — without requiring access to institutional-grade minimums or complexity.

Capital Preservation Priority

Portfolio construction prioritizes preservation of principal. Downside risk management is hardwired into the instrument selection process.

Compounding Returns

Sukuk income is reinvested by default. Your capital base grows continuously rather than distributing yield as flat periodic payments.

0% Entry Fees

No subscription fee at entry. Every dirham invested goes directly to work in the portfolio from day one.

Investment-Grade Only

Only investment-grade Sukuk instruments qualify for inclusion. High-yield or speculative-grade bonds are excluded from every portfolio class.

Investment Thesis

Where we see durable value.

Keheilan's venture strategy targets structural gaps in the MENA market, driving durable economic value through deep technology.

0101
Barriers to entry are structurally high

Deep technology ventures require specialized expertise, significant capital, and long development cycles, creating natural moats that compound over time.

0202
Local innovation is underdeveloped but strategically important

The region's technical talent and research infrastructure are growing faster than capital deployment, creating a durable opportunity for early conviction.

0303
Government, enterprise, and market demand are structurally aligned

Policy priorities, corporate needs, and consumer demand increasingly converge around technological self-sufficiency, de-risking the category over time.

How We Invest

Deliberate. Focused. Deeply involved.

Our investment approach is built around concentrated conviction, active partnership, and long-term value creation.

01
Selective by Design

We deploy capital into a limited number of high-conviction investments. Deal volume is not a goal. Each position we take reflects deep diligence and genuine conviction in the team and category.

02
Early to Growth Stages

Our focus spans pre-seed through Series A, with the ability to lead or co-lead rounds and support follow-on growth. We enter early enough to shape, not just back.

03
Regional First, Global Potential

We back companies solving regional problems with the potential to scale beyond local markets. MENA is the starting point, not the ceiling.

04
Active Ownership

We work closely with founders on strategy, governance, and access to capital, while maintaining disciplined boundaries. Partnership without overreach.

SFDR Disclosure — Article 8 Classification

Keheilan Asset Management is registered in the Netherlands and subject to SFDR (EU) 2019/2088. All strategies are classified as Article 8 — they promote social and ethical characteristics by constructing portfolios within a Shariah-compliant investment universe (excluding interest-bearing instruments, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, and gambling). This is a structural feature of instrument selection, not a post-hoc screen. Keheilan does not commit to a minimum proportion of EU Taxonomy-aligned investments and does not represent its strategies as "sustainable investments" under Article 2(17) of SFDR. Principal adverse impacts (PAIs) are not currently considered at the entity level under Article 4. For more information: invest@keheilan.com

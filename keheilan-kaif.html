Shariah Framework

Principles you can rely on. Built into the structure.

KAIF's Shariah compliance is not a post-screening filter applied to an otherwise conventional portfolio. It is embedded in the investment process from initial selection through ongoing monitoring.

Prohibited Sectors Excluded

Alcohol, tobacco, weapons, conventional finance, adult entertainment — all systematically screened out at the universe level.

Debt Ratio Compliance

Companies with total debt exceeding 33% of total assets are excluded. Leverage constraints are applied to every position.

Continuous Monitoring

Compliance is monitored on an ongoing basis — not just at entry. Positions that drift out of compliance are flagged and replaced systematically.

Revenue Screening

Non-compliant revenue must be less than 5% of total revenue. Income from interest, gambling, and impermissible activities is tracked and excluded.
